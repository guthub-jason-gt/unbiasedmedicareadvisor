What Is a Fee-Only Medicare Advisor?

A fee-only Medicare advisor is a consultant who charges you directly for their expertise and does not receive any commissions, referral fees, or compensation from insurance companies. This model eliminates conflicts of interest and ensures that the advice you receive is truly unbiased.

When you work with a fee-only advisor, you can trust that our recommendations are based solely on what's best for your situation — not on which plan pays us the highest commission.

Fee-Only vs Commission-Based Advisors

Fee-Only Advisor Our Model

  • You pay us directly, so we work for you
  • No commissions or kickbacks from insurers
  • We recommend ALL options, not just ones that pay us
  • Transparent, upfront pricing
  • No incentive to push expensive or unnecessary plans
  • Advice based solely on your needs

Commission-Based Agent

  • "Free" help paid for by insurance companies
  • Earns commissions when you enroll in certain plans
  • May only show plans that pay commissions
  • Hidden financial incentives
  • May push higher-commission plans
  • Advice may be influenced by compensation
