Our Comparison Process

A thorough, personalized approach to finding your ideal Medicare coverage.

1

Gather Your Information

We start by understanding your complete healthcare picture: current doctors, prescription medications, health conditions, budget preferences, and coverage priorities. This ensures our comparison is tailored to your actual needs.

2

Research Available Plans

We research every plan available in your zip code across all carriers. This includes checking provider networks, running your medications through drug formularies, and calculating true annual costs.

3

Build Your Comparison Report

We compile our findings into a clear, easy-to-understand comparison report. You'll see side-by-side plan details, total estimated costs, and how each option meets your specific requirements.

4

Review Together

We walk through the comparison with you, explaining the trade-offs between plans and answering all your questions. You'll understand exactly what you're getting with each option.

5

Support Your Decision

Once you've chosen the plan that's right for you, we help ensure a smooth enrollment process. No pressure, no sales tactics - just clear support for the choice you make.

Factors We Analyze

Our comparison goes beyond premiums to examine what really matters for your healthcare.

Total Annual Costs

We calculate premiums, deductibles, copays, and coinsurance to show your true yearly healthcare expenses with each plan.

Doctor Access

We verify your current doctors and specialists are in-network, and assess how easy it is to see new providers if needed.

Drug Coverage

Your specific medications are analyzed for formulary coverage, tier placement, and total drug costs throughout the year.

Hospital Networks

We check which hospitals and facilities are covered, especially important for those with ongoing care needs.

Extra Benefits

Dental, vision, hearing, fitness programs, and other extras are compared across Medicare Advantage plans.

Travel Coverage

For those who travel, we assess how each plan covers care outside your home area domestically and abroad.

Financial Stability

We review carrier ratings and rate increase history so you know your plan will be there when you need it.

Flexibility

We examine referral requirements, prior authorizations, and how much freedom you have to direct your own care.
