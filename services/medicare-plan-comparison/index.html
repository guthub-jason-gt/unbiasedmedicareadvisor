Medicare Plan Comparison Service

Cut through the confusion with a personalized, side-by-side comparison of Medicare plans in your area. We analyze the details so you can make a confident choice.

Why Plan Comparison Matters

Most areas have 50+ Medicare plan options. Without a comprehensive comparison, you're essentially guessing — and that guess could cost you thousands of dollars annually in premiums and out-of-pocket expenses.

Our independent plan comparison service analyzes every option available to you and presents clear, side-by-side comparisons tailored to your specific healthcare needs.

What We Compare

Medicare Supplement (Medigap)

Plans A, B, C, D, F, G, K, L, M, N

  • ✓ Monthly premium costs by carrier
  • ✓ Coverage gaps filled by each plan
  • ✓ Out-of-pocket maximums
  • ✓ Carrier financial ratings
  • ✓ Rate increase history
  • ✓ Household discounts available

Medicare Advantage (Part C)

HMO, PPO, PFFS, SNP Plans

  • ✓ Monthly premiums and copays
  • ✓ Doctor and hospital networks
  • ✓ Drug coverage (Part D included)
  • ✓ Extra benefits (dental, vision, hearing)
  • ✓ Star ratings and quality scores
  • ✓ Prior authorization requirements

Part D Prescription Plans

Standalone Drug Coverage

  • ✓ Monthly premiums
  • ✓ Deductibles and copays
  • ✓ Your specific drug costs
  • ✓ Formulary tier placement
  • ✓ Pharmacy network options
  • ✓ Coverage gap (donut hole) costs
