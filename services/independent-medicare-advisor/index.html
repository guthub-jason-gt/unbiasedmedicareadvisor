What Independence Really Means

Our independence isn't just a talking point - it's the foundation of how we serve you.

🔒

No Insurance Company Ties

We have no contracts, quotas, or financial arrangements with any insurance carrier. This means we can recommend the best plan for your situation, not the one that pays us the most.

💰

Fee-Only Compensation

You pay us directly for our expertise, just like you'd pay a lawyer or accountant. We don't receive commissions, bonuses, or incentives from insurance companies.

🤝

Your Advocate First

As your independent advisor, we work exclusively for you. Our loyalty is to helping you find the right coverage, not to meeting sales targets or pushing specific products.

📈

Unbiased Analysis

We analyze all available options using objective criteria. Whether it's Medicare Supplement, Medicare Advantage, or Part D plans, we evaluate each on its merits for your specific needs.

📚

Education-Focused

We believe in empowering you with knowledge, not pressuring you into quick decisions. Take the time you need to understand your options fully before choosing.

🏠

Long-Term Relationship

We're here for the long haul. As your needs change, we'll help you evaluate whether your current coverage still makes sense or if adjustments would benefit you.

What to Expect Working With Us

Our independent advisory process puts your needs at the center of every decision.

1

Discovery Call

We start with a free, no-obligation consultation to understand your health needs, budget, preferred doctors, and prescription medications.

2

Comprehensive Analysis

We research all available options in your area, comparing benefits, costs, provider networks, and drug coverage against your specific requirements.

3

Personalized Recommendations

You receive a clear, written comparison of your top options with our independent assessment of which plans best fit your situation and why.

4

Decision Support

We answer all your questions, explain the trade-offs, and give you the time you need to make a confident choice. No pressure, ever.

Enrollment Assistance

Ongoing Partnership

We're here for you year-round. Whether you have questions about using your benefits or want an annual review of your coverage, we're just a call away.
