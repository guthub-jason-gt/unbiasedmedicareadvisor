What Does Independence Mean?

An independent Medicare advisor has no contracts, quotas, or financial arrangements with any insurance carrier. This means we can recommend the best plan for your situation — not the one that pays us the most.

When you work with an independent advisor, you can trust that our recommendations are based solely on what's best for you. We're your advocate first, always.

Independent vs Commission-Based Advisors

Independent Advisor Our Model

  • Primary loyalty is to you, the client
  • Paid directly by you (fee-only)
  • Recommendations based solely on your needs
  • Full transparency and disclosure
  • No pressure to enroll quickly
  • Can review any plan from any carrier
  • Long-term relationship focus

Commission-Based Agent

  • Primary loyalty to insurance carriers
  • Paid by insurers through commissions
  • May favor high-commission plans
  • Conflicts of interest often unclear
  • Pressure to enroll is common
  • Limited to contracted carriers only
  • Focused on initial sale
