About Us

Independent, fee-only Medicare guidance you can trust. We work for you, not insurance companies.

Why We Started Unbiased Medicare Advisor

Medicare is confusing. With dozens of plans, complicated rules, and high-pressure sales tactics from insurance agents, it's hard to know who to trust. Most "free" Medicare help comes from agents who earn commissions when you sign up for certain plans — plans that may not be the best fit for you.

We started Unbiased Medicare Advisor to offer something different: honest, independent Medicare guidance with no hidden agenda. As a fee-only service, we don't sell insurance policies or earn commissions. Our only job is to help you understand your options and make the best decision for your unique situation.

What Makes Us Different

💰

Fee-Only Model

You pay us directly for our time and expertise. We never receive commissions or referral fees from insurance companies.

All Options Reviewed

How We Help You

Whether you're turning 65 and new to Medicare, or you've been enrolled for years and want to review your options, we're here to help. Our consultations cover:

What We Cover in a Consultation

Understanding your options: Medicare Parts A, B, C, and D, Medigap plans, Medicare Advantage plans, and how they all work together.

Comparing plans: Side-by-side analysis of plans available in your area, including costs, coverage, and provider networks.

Enrollment guidance: When to enroll, how to avoid penalties, and step-by-step help with the process.

Ongoing questions: Even after enrollment, we're here to answer questions as they come up.

Our Commitment to You

We believe everyone deserves access to unbiased Medicare information. That's why we offer free educational resources on our website, including comprehensive guides and state-specific information. When you need personalized help, our consultation services are available for a transparent, flat fee.

No sales pressure. No hidden incentives. Just honest answers to your Medicare questions.
